About Total Rush

Total Rush challenges players to select numbered tiles that add up to a target total before time runs out. Build streaks, unlock themes, and compete for high scores.

Features Planned for V1

  • Fast arcade-style number puzzle gameplay
  • Streak-based scoring
  • Unlockable visual themes
  • Leaderboards
  • Optional rewarded ads and remove-ads purchase
